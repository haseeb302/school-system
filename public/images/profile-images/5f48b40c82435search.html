      Joan Elisabeth Lowther Murray, MBE was an English cryptanalyst and numismatist best known for her work as a code-breaker at Bletchley Park during the Second World War. Wikipedia
      Born: June 24, 1917, West Norwood, London, United Kingdom
      Died: September 4, 1996, Headington, Oxford, United Kingdom
      Spouse: John Kenneth Ronald Murray (m. 19521986)
      Partner: Alan Turing (engaged during 1941 but did not marry)
      Awards: British Numismatic Society Sanford Saltus Gold Medal (1986)
      Education: Newnham College, University of Cambridge
